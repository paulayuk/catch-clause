Data Protection Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At SecureApp, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services effectively.

Data We Collect

We collect only essential information required for service functionality:

  • Email address for account creation and notifications
  • Username for account identification
  • Service usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Technical logs for security and performance monitoring

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing and improving our service functionality
  • Sending important service notifications
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention
  • Technical support when requested

We do not use your data for marketing purposes or share it with third parties for advertising.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

We use minimal third-party services, limited to essential infrastructure providers with strong privacy protections.

Cookies and Tracking

We use only essential cookies required for service functionality:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
auth_token User authentication Session Strictly Necessary
csrf_token Security protection Session Strictly Necessary
user_settings Remember user preferences 30 days Functional

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics services.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: Until account deletion
  • Usage logs: 30 days maximum
  • Security logs: 90 days maximum
  • Deleted account data: Permanently removed within 30 days

Your Data Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a standard format

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

  1. Log into your account and visit the "Privacy Controls" section
  2. Use the self-service data management tools
  3. For account deletion, use the "Delete Account" button
  4. For assistance, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@secureapp.com

Response time: We respond to all data requests within 24 hours and complete data deletion within 30 days.

Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, regular security audits, and strict access controls. All data is stored in secure data centers with appropriate physical and digital protections.
